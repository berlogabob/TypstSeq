  TL;DR

  The "working strange" isn't lag — the vault never opens, and the app hides it.
  On your phone the vault at /sdcard/TyLog is healthy (1390 notes: 1373
  articles, 11 daily, 4 notes, 2 projects, zero sync conflicts), but openVault
  stalls forever inside _readPkms (workspace_controller.dart:506) — the
  search-index build does sequential per-file SAF reads over up to 1373 articles
  with no timeout (tylog_core/src/search_index.dart:147). Proof from the
  device: index.json gets rewritten on every launch, search-index.json.gz hasn't
  been written since 09:22, and the process sits idle at 0% CPU. Since the UI
  only gets data after the whole pipeline finishes, every screen shows a fake
  "No journal pages yet" / "Start writing…" — the only honest text on the entire
  phone is "Vault not open" buried in the sync dashboard. A trigger: the app
  was uninstalled/reinstalled today at 07:44, which silently dropped the SAF
  grant (vault files still belong to the old install's uid).

  Two data-safety issues found along the way: typing while the vault is closed
  is silently discarded (save() no-ops, workspace_controller.dart:177), and the
  picker flow once initialized a stray vault inside your Audiobooks folder —
  there's no confirmation step after folder selection.

  Highest-leverage fix (also fixes most of the "lag"): show content from the
  already-persisted index.json immediately, rebuild indexes in the background,
  and put a timeout + visible error/Retry on every SAF call. The report ranks
  everything else: the 5 eager ListViews, the global setState on a 346-line
  build(), main-thread Typst render, the 700 ms autosave debounce.

  UX audit (Nielsen + Fitts/Hick/Jakob/Doherty, 3 haiku subagents): severity-4s
  are the silent failure masking, the adjacent "Forget vault"/"Delete vault and
  files" menu, and the unconfirmed folder pick. Also: Settings says "Sync: Not
  configured" while the dashboard shows your configured Nextcloud; Settings
  itself is only reachable by scrolling the More sheet; the sixth Library tab
  truncates to "En"; and there's a literal "Typst help" typo
  (app_mobile.dart:2787). The PKMS section suggests: quick-capture FAB (capture
  is 4+ taps today), [[wikilink]] sugar over #tylog.ref-note(...), promoting
  backlinks out of the More menu, and replacing the 6-tab Library with one
  filtered stream.

  One note: real frame-jank numbers weren't measurable — the release build hides
  Flutter frames from gfxinfo, and installing the profile APK would have
  required an uninstall (wiping your vault registry/keystore), which I wasn't
  willing to do. The report includes a one-line signing change that makes
  profiling possible without data loss. I left the phone with TyLog foregrounded
  and re-granted folder access; nothing in your vault was modified.
